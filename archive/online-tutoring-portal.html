Tutors On Wheels' Collaboration Portal is now Online!

Learn more about our online tutoring platform.

Requirements Checklist
  • Browser with Adobe FlashPlayer Installed
  • Reliable Internet Connection. (Check your internet speed by clicking here. If your “Jitter” score is above 150 ms, you are likely to have trouble with audio or video lag or you may lose your connection completely.)
  • Headphones and Microphone (plugged in before start of session)
  • Web Cam (optional)
  • Username & Password*

It is a good idea to log in and check out the system, prior to your first tutoring appointment. Get some idea of how it works by entering our demo room.

*Users must be preregistered with Tutors on Wheels in order to use the collaboration portal. If you do not have your registration info, please contact an administrator at Tutors On Wheels. Students and guests should receive an email with a unique link for their sessions.
