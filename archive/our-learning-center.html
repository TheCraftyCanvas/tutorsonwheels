The design of our learning center is based on a comfortable home-like enviorment.

 


At Tutors on Wheels we know that learnnig begins the moment you step into our learning center. We know a richlearning atmosphere will effect student's learning attitude, so we have made sure our rooms are pleasant and fun.

The colors of our wall are neutral to induce calmness. The windows create an open feeling and reduce anxiety. Our walls promote a learning atmosphere with many educational and motivational posters.

The learning center also offers spring water, juice and balanced healthy snacks to the students. The learning center has a comfortable area for parents who come tires from work as well as tea and coffee.


 


Your child will receive the highest-quality one-to-one instruction available today. We will help your child develop stronger academic skills, earn better grades, and score higher on standardized tests. The individualized instruction will also help your child gain confidence, motivation, and improved concentration.
